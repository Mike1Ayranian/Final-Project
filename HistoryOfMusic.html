Some of Music's Most Famous Artists

James Brown

The Godfather of Soul

Born in Barnwell, South Carolina, on May 3, 1933, into extreme poverty, James Brown worked his way to the top of the funk and R&B music, singing songs such as "It's a Man's, Man's, Man's World" and "I Feel Good", earning the nickname "The Godfather of Soul."

Check him out live here!

Elvis Presley

The King of Rock and Roll

Elvis Aaron Presley was born to his parents, Vernon and Gladys Presley, in his home in Tupelo, Mississippi, on January 8, 1935. His brother, Jessie Garon, was stillborn, leaving Elvis to grow up as an only child. He was an American singer and actor, regarded as one of the most important cultural icons of the 20th century.He is often referred to as "The King of Rock and Roll", or just as "The King"
